About Mac Wayne Battered Coin

Mac Wayne Battered Coin (MWB) is the world's first cryptocurrency specifically designed to support and advance accessibility technology innovation.

Created to bridge the gap between blockchain technology and assistive devices, MWB tokens directly fund partnerships with leading accessibility companies including MetaView, Aris Technologies, Google Accessibility, and Microsoft Accessibility teams.

Every transaction contributes to research and development of screen readers, adaptive interfaces, and universal design solutions that make technology accessible to everyone.
